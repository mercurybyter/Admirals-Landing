Discover Farragut

Full of History, Fun, and Community

Farragut is a diverse town named after Admiral David Farragut, who was born in today's Campbell Station. With a population of over 26,000, Farragut offers many places to explore the beautiful nature of East Tennessee and engage in the local community.
